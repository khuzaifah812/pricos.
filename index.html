Welcome to PRIDE COLLEGE SCHOOL - MPIGI

PRIDE COLLEGE SCHOOL - MPIGI is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, discipline, leadership and innovation. Our school prepares students to become responsible citizens capable of competing globally.

We provide a conducive learning environment supported by qualified teachers, modern ICT laboratories, a well-equipped library, science laboratories and various co-curricular activities that promote holistic development.

Principal's Welcome

A Message from the Principal

Welcome to PRIDE COLLEGE SCHOOL - MPIGI. We are delighted that you have chosen to learn more about our school. Our commitment is to provide every learner with a quality education that inspires excellence, discipline, creativity, leadership and lifelong learning.

We believe that every student possesses unique talents and abilities. Through dedicated teaching, modern learning facilities and strong moral values, we empower our students to become confident, responsible and successful members of society.

We warmly invite you to become part of our vibrant school community where academic excellence and character development go hand in hand.
